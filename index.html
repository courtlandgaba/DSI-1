our story

Document Solutions, Inc. was founded in 1989. The company was founded with the goal of offering a competitive product with the best service and support in the area. Tremendeous growth has been seen during the recent years but the goals of DSI have remained the same.

DSI has become the only vendor in the area to offer a full service package. With a staff of 45 employees DSI can offer an incredibly wide variation of both services and products and can do so without the use of any subcontractors. The service/support and integration group of DSI make up one of the largest in four state area.

These services range from complete business integration, providing the latest in Digital duplication to the latest in computer technology and network design. With experience in Local and Wide Area Networks and variety of network operating systems, DSI can provide a custom installation to fit the customer's needs. Dsi offers the latest and most up to date copier technology in the quad-state area.

DSI employs both certified Network Engineers as well as Certified Network Technicians along with Certified copier technicians.
